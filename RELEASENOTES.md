# Zuno Release Notes - v1.0.1+2

## Welcome to the Initial Beta Release! 🚀

Zuno is an AI-powered relationship companion designed to help couples navigate their emotional landscape together. This version marks our first official testing release.

---

### 🌟 High-Level Features

#### 1. The Digital Hearth (Dashboard)
- **Mood Tracking**: Log your daily emotions with a beautiful, intuitive interface.
- **Streak System**: Database-backed streak tracking to encourage consistent emotional mindfulness.
- **Daily Check-ins**: Quick logs for mood and journaling to keep your partner in the loop.

#### 2. Advanced Cycle Tracking
- **Interactive Calendar**: Visualize your cycle with an elegant heatmap and period history.
- **Cycle History Bar Charts**: Monitor your cycle regularity with historical duration comparisons.
- **AI-Powered Predictions**: Smarter forecasting of upcoming cycles.

#### 3. Personal & Shared Insights
- **Gemini-Powered AI**: Receive personalized daily and cycle insights based on your logs.
- **Multi-Language Support**: AI content available in **English**, **Malayalam**, and **Kannada**.
- **Privacy Controls**: "Private Journal Notes" toggle to keep specific reflections sensitive and excluded from shared partner insights.

#### 4. Secure Partner Sync
- **QR-Based Pairing**: Fast and secure connection with your partner.
- **Realtime Updates**: Instant unpairing/pairing detection across both devices using Supabase Realtime.
- **Relationship Management**: Manage your combined profile effortlessly.
